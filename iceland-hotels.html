The langoustine dinner — a logistics tip

Iceland's legendary lobster-soup spot is Fjöruborðið in Stokkseyri — but it's ~50 min west of your base, so a ferry-night run is a slog. Best move: eat there on Fri Jun 26 on the drive in — Stokkseyri sits right on the route from Keflavík, so it costs almost no extra driving. Keep ferry-night dinner (Jun 27) near base.
